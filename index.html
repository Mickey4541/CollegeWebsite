ABOUT US

REC, established in 2008, offers cutting-edge engineering and IT education in Nepal's Mid-Western Region. Affiliated with Pokhara University, it provides BCA since 2010, and Civil Engineering and Electronics & Communication since 2011. Additionally, REC offers diploma and TSLC programs in various engineering disciplines under CTEVT affiliation.
